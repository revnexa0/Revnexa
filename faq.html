General

About Our Services

What services does Revnexa offer?+

Revnexa provides comprehensive app reputation growth services including user feedback collection, app testing & insights, reputation growth strategy, review management, community engagement, and performance analytics. We help apps of all sizes improve their ratings and build lasting trust.

How is Revnexa different from other services?+

Unlike other services, Revnexa focuses exclusively on authentic user engagement. We never use bots, fake accounts, or artificial methods. Every piece of feedback comes from real users who genuinely interact with your app. This ensures sustainable growth that won't put your app at risk.

What types of apps do you work with?+

We work with apps across all categories — from e-commerce and gaming to fintech, healthcare, travel, and more. Whether you're a startup launching your first app or an enterprise managing a portfolio, we have solutions tailored to your needs.

Process

How It Works

How quickly can I see results?+

Most clients see measurable improvements within the first 2-4 weeks. However, sustainable reputation growth is a continuous process. We recommend a minimum 3-month commitment for the best results, though many clients see significant improvements much sooner.

What does the onboarding process look like?+

1) Free consultation call to understand your needs. 2) We analyze your app's current reputation and market position. 3) We create a custom strategy and present it to you. 4) Once approved, we begin implementation with regular progress updates.

Is your approach compliant with app store guidelines?+

Absolutely. All our methods are 100% compliant with both Google Play Store and Apple App Store guidelines. We focus on authentic user engagement and never employ any techniques that could put your app at risk. Your app's safety is our top priority.

Pricing & Plans

Pricing Questions

Do you offer a free trial or consultation?+

Yes! We offer a completely free initial consultation where we analyze your app's current reputation, identify growth opportunities, and provide actionable recommendations — no commitment required. This helps you understand the potential before investing.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?+

Yes, all our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts required. You can cancel anytime. However, we recommend at least a 3-month commitment to see the full impact of our strategies.

Do you offer custom plans?+

Absolutely. Our Enterprise plan is fully customizable to meet your specific needs. Whether you need higher volume, specific market focus, or custom integrations, we'll create a plan that works for you. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

Support

Getting Help

How can I contact your support team?+

You can reach us through multiple channels: email, WhatsApp, Telegram, or our website's live chat feature. Professional and Enterprise plan clients also get priority support with dedicated account managers.

What reporting do you provide?+

We provide detailed reports that include rating trends, feedback summaries, sentiment analysis, competitive benchmarking, and actionable insights. Report frequency depends on your plan: weekly (Starter), daily (Professional), or real-time (Enterprise).
